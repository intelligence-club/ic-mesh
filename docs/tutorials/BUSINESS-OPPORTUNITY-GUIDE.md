# IC Mesh: The Decentralized AI Business Opportunity

**Why Smart Money is Moving to Distributed AI Networks**

The AI revolution created a $200 billion market dominated by a few tech giants. IC Mesh democratizes this opportunity, letting **anyone** participate in the AI economy - either as a customer saving 60-80% on AI services, or as an operator earning passive income from spare computing resources.

### The AI Services Market Problem

**Traditional AI APIs are expensive**:
- OpenAI Whisper API: $6.00 per hour of audio
- Google Cloud Speech-to-Text: $2.16 per hour  
- Amazon Transcribe: $2.40 per hour
- Azure Speech Services: $3.00 per hour

**IC Mesh pricing**:
- Transcription: $0.30-0.50 per hour (83-91% savings)

**Why the massive cost difference?**
- No data center overhead
- No corporate profit margins
- Direct peer-to-peer pricing
- Utilizes existing hardware efficiently
