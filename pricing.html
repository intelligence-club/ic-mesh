❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is an "int"?
An "int" is our unit of compute time: 1 int = 1 second of processing. Different tasks use different amounts based on complexity. A 60-second audio transcription uses ~60-75 ints, while generating an image uses ~200-400 ints.
Do my credits expire?
No! Your ints never expire. Buy once, use whenever you need. This isn't a subscription service — you only pay for what you actually use.
How accurate is the transcription?
We use OpenAI's Whisper model running on our distributed network, which delivers the same accuracy as the Whisper API but at significantly lower cost. Accuracy is typically 95%+ for clear audio in supported languages.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Your files are encrypted during transfer and processing. Network operators can't access your raw data — they only process encrypted jobs. All results are delivered directly to you via email or API callback.
What file formats are supported?
Audio: MP3, WAV, M4A, FLAC, OGG • Images: PNG, JPG, WebP • Documents: PDF, PNG, JPG for OCR. Maximum file size is 100MB per upload.
Can I get a refund?
Yes! We offer full refunds within 30 days of purchase if you're not satisfied. Unused ints can be refunded at any time. Contact support through your account page.
How fast is processing?
Processing speed depends on network capacity and job complexity. Typical times: Audio transcription (2-5 minutes), Image generation (1-3 minutes), Document OCR (30-60 seconds). Jobs are processed in order of arrival.
Can I use this for commercial projects?
Absolutely! There are no restrictions on commercial use. Many developers integrate our API into their applications for cost-effective AI processing. Full API documentation is available.
